About

Who we are

Live.Love.Believe (Love Africa) founded in 2012, operates as a non-profit organisation based in Barkley West, Northeren Cape (SA).

  • As a non-profit organization, Love Africa:
  • Conceptualizes, promotes, and participates in projects.
  • Focuses on sustainable socio-economic development, education, and digital inclusion.
  • Works co-creatively to empower communities.

We are dedicated to the sustainable development of our communities, including the protection of the environment. We recognize the connection between the environment, energy, education, and health issues. These factors often drive socio-economic and environmental degradation.

Our goal is to provide innovative, practical, and sustainable solutions to community problems. We align our efforts with the framework of the sustainable development goals.
