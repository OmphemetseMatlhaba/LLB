About LLB Africa

Live.Love.Believe (Love Africa), founded in 2012, operates as a non-profit organisation based in Barkley West, South Africa. We are dedicated to the sustainable development of our communities, including the protection of the environment. Recognizing the correlation between the environment, energy, education, and health, these factors often drive socio-economic and environmental degradation.

As a non-profit organization, Love Africa conceptualizes, promotes, and participates in projects that drive sustainable socio-economic development, education, and digital inclusion. Our quest is co-creative, aiming to empower communities through innovative, practical, and sustainable solutions aligned with the sustainable development goals.



Our Vision

A robust circular economy with reduced or no environmental harm, in which innovation and sustainable development thrive with the efficient management of resources, for the benefit of youth and the environment providing a better quality of life for communities.

Our Mission

Love Africa focuses on sustainable development of communities and youths. As an organisation LLB Africa supports youths to be actively involved in the innovation, design, and implementation of solutions to the issues faced by communities.

  • As a non-profit organization, Love Africa:
  • Working together to influence social, economic, and environmental systems
  • Improving and building sustainable capabilities of the youth, predominantly women.
  • Increase access to knowledge, skills development, and educational opportunities
  • Promote participation in sustainable community development projects, driving socio economic development efforts to address myriad of challenges facing the youth.

Our Values

In addition to our values, embedded in our organisation name, the below are values we embody when dealing with all our clients:

  • Sustainability
  • Community Focus
  • Knowledge & skills sharing
  • Humanity
  • Love

Our Approach

Our approach engages with communities to understand the culture and environment in which they live or work. We engage and work with different stakeholders in our efforts towards the following universal goals:

  • As a non-profit organization, Love Africa:
  • Sustainable access to clean energy
  • Quality education
  • Environmental conservation
  • Sustainability Zero Hunger
